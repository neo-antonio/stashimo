Pantry Tracker

Add food and supplies, tag them with a custom color and name, and group them however you like. Mark items as eaten/used, out of stock, or needs refilling with an optional target count.

Meal Planner

Plan meals for any day of the week and list the ingredients you'll need. Each meal shows whether its ingredients are complete, partial, or missing.

Browse other weeks with the calendar, and check off ingredients from the Grocery List as you shop.
