Supplies Tracker

Add food and supplies, group them by a custom-colored type, and track a unit + price per unit. Mark items as used, out of stock, or needs refilling with an optional target count.

Meal Planner

Plan meals for any day of the week and list the ingredients you'll need. Each meal shows whether its ingredients are complete, partial, or missing.

Meals repeat weekly — editing ingredients applies from that week forward, leaving past weeks untouched. Check off items right from the Grocery List as you shop.
