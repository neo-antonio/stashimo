Supplies Tracker

Add food and supplies, group them by a custom-colored type, and track a unit and price. Each card has quick buttons to knock down stock, jump straight to a percent, or flag an item as needing a refill or out of stock — tap a status button again to clear it once you've restocked.

Bulk Pricing & Restock Cost

If an item can only be bought in bulk (e.g. ₱300 for a pack of 6), set Min Price and Min Units to that pack price and quantity. The Restock Cost Estimate then rounds up to full packs, since you can't buy a single unit on its own.

For things like shampoo or lotion, check "Measure percent of last stock" to track fullness of just the last bottle. It stays at 100% while 2 or more are in stock, and you can set a target percent for when it should flag as needing refill.

Meal Planner

Plan meals for any day of the week and list the ingredients you'll need. Each meal card shows how many ingredients are bought — tap Edit to see or check off the full list.

Meals belong to one week only. If you type the same ingredient name into more than one meal, it's treated as one shared item — buying it checks it off for every meal that uses it. Reuse a past meal anytime and pick which day to place it on. Check off items in the Grocery List as you shop.

Distributor

Set a Plan Date, then log expected income and planned expenses to see what's left. Mark a purchase as debt or credit card, and it counts as income too — since it's financed rather than paid from cash on hand, Money Left won't go negative just because it's on credit.

Use Copy Plan or Export Plan anytime to save a quick reference of the whole tab.
